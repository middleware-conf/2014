Registration

Conference fees are given in the table below. All fees are in Euro (EUR). Discounted fees are available for students and members of ACM. The early registration prices end on October 24 October 30, 2014 (23:59 UTC/GMT -12h).

A non-student registration fee (member or non-member) is required for each accepted paper at the main conference, even if the presenter would qualify for student fees. For a presentation at the doctoral symposium or a workshop a student fee is applicable. At least one author of each accepted paper must be registered before the early registration deadline (October 30) for paper inclusion in the proceedings.

Online Registration

Request for an official invitation letter for your visa application must be done during the registration process (please check the corresponding box in the registration form). You will receive an electronic version of your official letter few days after your registration has been completed. For additional information or specific request (such as an original hard copy), please contact registration desk.

Due to technical difficulties the Web registration process was out of service for several hours. To accomadate that, the early registration deadline is extended to October 30, 2014 (23:59 UTC/GMT -12h). We apologize for the inconvenience.
